A Little Goes A Long Way!

Charity:water is committed to bringing clean and safe drinking water to people around the world. Every dollar you give helps fund community-owned water projects in developing countries, and we prove every project you help fund with photos and GPS coordinates.

Your support transforms lives—when a community gains access to clean water, it can change everything: improved health, more time for education, and new opportunities for families to thrive.

Why Water?

785 million people lack basic access to clean and safe drinking water. Charity:water exists to change that. We partner with local organizations to build sustainable, community-led solutions that last. By giving today, you’re joining a movement that believes clean water is a basic human right.

Since 2006, we’ve funded thousands of water projects around the globe, bringing clean water to millions of people. And we’re just getting started.

Health

Clean water reduces disease and saves lives, creating healthier communities everywhere.

Education

With access to water, children—especially girls—can spend more time in school and less time walking for water.

Opportunity

Clean water unlocks new potential, allowing families to thrive with better work and economic opportunities.
